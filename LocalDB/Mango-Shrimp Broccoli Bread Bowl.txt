Mango-Shrimp Broccoli Bread Bowl

• 1 mango, diced• 12 cooked shrimp• 6 cups steamed broccoli florets• 1 1/2 cups of breadcrumbs• 1/4 cup of melted butter• Salt and pepper to taste• 2 tablespoons of olive oil
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.2. In a large bowl combine the diced mango, cooked shrimp, steamed broccoli, and breadcrumbs.3. Drizzle the melted butter and olive oil over the mixture, season with salt and pepper.4. Mix together until all ingredients are evenly coated.5. Grease four oven-safe ramekins or baking dishes.6. Divide the mixture evenly into each ramekin and press down to compact slightly.7. Bake for 18 to 20 minutes until the top is golden-brown.8. Serve warm and enjoy!
