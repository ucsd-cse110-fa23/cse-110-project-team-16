Beef and Potato Bake
images/Beef and Potato Bake.jpg
Dinner
beef, potato

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
2. Grease an oven-safe dish.
3. Place the beef in the bottom of the dish.
4. Peel and cube the potato.
5. Spread the potato cubes out across the beef.
6. Drizzle oil over the potatoes.
7. Sprinkle salt and any other desired seasonings over the potatoes.
8. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until the potatoes and beef are cooked.
