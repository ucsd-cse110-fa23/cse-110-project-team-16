Pasta Alfredo with Sausage
images/Pasta Alfredo with Sausage.jpg
Lunch
Pasta. Sausage. Alfredo.

1. Cook the sausage according to package instructions.
2. Boil the pasta according to package instructions.
3. Once cooked, strain the pasta.
4. Put the cooked sausage in a large pan on medium-high heat.
5. Add Alfredo sauce to the pan, stirring to combine.
6. Add the cooked pasta to the pan, and stir to combine.
7. Serve and enjoy!
