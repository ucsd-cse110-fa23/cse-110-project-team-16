Chicken and Rice with Onions 
Breakfast
 - 1 tablespoon olive oil - 1 large onion, diced - 2 cloves of garlic, minced - 2 cups cooked white rice - 2 cups cooked, shredded chicken - 2 cups low-sodium chicken broth - 1 teaspoon cumin - 1 teaspoon paprika - Salt and pepper to taste 
 1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat until shimmering. 2. Add the onion and garlic and cook, stirring often, until the onion is soft and translucent, about 5 minutes. 3. Add the rice, chicken, chicken broth, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper. Stir everything together and bring to a low boil. 4. Once boiling, reduce heat to low and cover the pot. Simmer until the rice is cooked through, about 20 minutes. 5. Serve warm. Enjoy!
