Breakfast Burrito
images/Breakfast Burrito.jpg
Breakfast
Eggs, bacon, cheese, tortilla.
 
1. Preheat a skillet on the stove over medium heat.
2. Place bacon in the skillet and cook until crisp, flipping over once.
3.Once fully cooked, place the bacon on a plate with a paper towel to collect the grease.
4. In a separate bowl, whisk together eggs.
5. Place a tortilla on the skillet and evenly distribute the eggs, bacon, and cheese on the tortilla.
6. Fold the sides of the tortilla so that they wrap the contents of the burrito.
7. Turn up the heat to medium-high and cook for a few minutes until the eggs are cooked through and the cheese is melted.
8. Serve with your favorite salsa and enjoy!
