Pizza
images/Pizza.jpg
Dinner
dough, pizza sauce, mozzarella, basil, pepperoni, parmesan.

1. Preheat oven to 375°
2. Roll out dough into a thin layer
3. Spread pizza sauce on dough
4. Sprinkle mozzarella and parmesan cheese on top
5. Place pepperoni and basil on top of cheese
6. Bake in preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and cheese is melted
