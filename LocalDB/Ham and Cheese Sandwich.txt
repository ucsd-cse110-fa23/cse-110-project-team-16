Ham and Cheese Sandwich
Dinner
Ham, bread, cheese, mayo, mustard, oil, vinegar.
 
1. Cut the bread into two medium-sized slices.
2. Put a thin layer of mustard on each slice of the bread.
3. Heat a pan on medium-high and add some oil.
4. On one side of the bread, place a few slices of ham and some slices of cheese.
5. Place the other piece of bread on top (mustard side down).
6. Cover the top and let it cook in the pan for a few minutes.
7. Flip the sandwich and let it cook for a few minutes on the other side.
8. Take the sandwich off the pan and spread mayo and vinegar on both sides.
9. Cut the sandwich in half and enjoy.
