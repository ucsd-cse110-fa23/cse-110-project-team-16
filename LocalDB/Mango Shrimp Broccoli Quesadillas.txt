Mango Shrimp Broccoli Quesadillas
Lunch
- 2 mangos, peeled and diced- 1 cup cooked shrimp, chopped- 1 cup steamed and chopped broccoli- 4 6-inch flour tortillas- 1/2 cup shredded Mexican blend cheese- 1 tablespoon olive oil- Tortilla chips, optional
1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.2. Add the mango, shrimp, and broccoli to the skillet and sauté for 2-3 minutes until shrimp is cooked through and the vegetables are tender.3. Place one of the tortillas in the skillet and top with half of the mango shrimp mixture. Sprinkle half of the cheese over the top.4. Place the second tortilla over the top and press down gently. Cook for 2 minutes, then flip and cook the other side. Repeat with the other two tortillas.5. Cut the quesadillas into quarters and serve with your favorite dip or salsa and extra cheese, if desired. Enjoy!
